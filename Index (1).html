We are Cait & Scott — two coaches, one mission. We built Clarity Coaching for the people who feel lost in the in-between, stuck at the crossroads, or quietly wondering if there's more out there for them.

The answer is yes. There is. We bring heart, experience, and a whole lot of soul to every session — creating a space where you feel truly seen, heard, and understood. Our style is warm, intuitive, and grounded, with just enough magic to remind you that you were never as lost as you thought.

You made it through the hard part. Now let's figure out what comes next.

Our Mission

To create a space where people feel truly seen, heard, and understood — and walk away with the clarity, courage, and direction to take their next step forward.

Our Vision

A world where no one stays stuck at the crossroads alone. Where every person knows they have the power within them to align with who they are, rise into who they're becoming, and move through life with purpose and confidence.

Disclaimer

Cait & Scott Clarity Coaching is a coaching practice, not a therapy or mental health service. Cait & Scott are not licensed therapists, psychologists, or medical professionals. Coaching is not a substitute for professional mental health treatment.

We work with clients who are past the acute phase of crisis and ready to focus on clarity, direction, and rebuilding. If you are currently in active crisis or need mental health support, we will help connect you with appropriate resources.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please seek support from a qualified professional immediately.
