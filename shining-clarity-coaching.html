Our Story

Two coaches. One experience built around you.

Scott and Cait work with every client together — bringing different strengths to the same table so nothing gets missed.

Most people come to coaching knowing something needs to change, but not quite how to get there. That's exactly where we work. Scott brings nearly two decades of experience helping people recognize the patterns quietly running their lives. Cait brings the kind of deep empathy that comes from lived experience — the ability to sit with someone in hard places and help them find their way through. Together, we build something neither of us could build alone: a coaching experience that's both clear-eyed and deeply human.

Scott

Scott has spent 18 years helping people see what they can't see on their own — the recurring patterns, the invisible systems, the habits that feel like personality but are actually just practice. His work is practical and grounded, but never cold. He knows that strategy without heart doesn't stick.

Cait

Cait's coaching is rooted in her own journey. That's not a talking point — it's what makes her different. She understands what it feels like to be in the middle of something hard with no clear way out, and she knows how to help people find their footing. Her approach is compassionate and action-oriented. Sitting with someone isn't the same as staying stuck.

Together

Every client gets both of us. We design your program together, we stay in communication throughout, and we bring our combined perspective to every step. You don't have to choose between structure and support — that's kind of the whole point.

Our Mission

To create a space where people feel truly seen, heard, and understood — and walk away with the clarity, courage, and direction to take their next step forward.

Our Vision

A world where no one stays stuck at the crossroads alone. Where every person knows they have the power within them to align with who they are, rise into who they're becoming, and move through life with purpose and confidence.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is coaching and how is it different from therapy?
Coaching is forward focused. We don't dig into your past — we work with where you are now and help you move toward where you want to be. Therapy is healing. Coaching is momentum.
How do I know which service is right for me?
Start with a free discovery call — that's exactly what it's for. We'll talk through what's going on and help you figure out the best fit together. No pressure, no commitment.
How do I get started?
Fill out the application on our Apply page and we'll be in touch to schedule your free discovery call.
Why are Mission Control spots limited?
Because you deserve more than a generic reply. Limiting spots means every single person gets real, personal attention during the daily launch window — not a rushed response lost in a crowd.
What does a session look like?
Every session is different because every person is different. We meet you where you are, ask the right questions, and help you find your own clarity. No scripts, no cookie cutter advice — just real conversation and real results.
Do you offer packages?
Yes — our Custom Coaching Package is designed around you. After your discovery call we'll put together a program that fits your timeline, goals, and life. Pricing is based on your custom program.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a full refund if you cancel before your first session. If you need to cancel within 48 hours of a session, a partial refund will be issued. We want this to feel safe and fair for everyone.
What are your business hours?
We'll share our current availability when you apply. Mission Control operates within dedicated daily windows — not 24/7 — so your messages always get the full attention they deserve.

Still have questions? Reach out — we're happy to help.

3. Our Services

We offer a free Discovery Call, Single Sessions at $125 per session, Custom Coaching Packages priced based on your program, and a Group Coaching waitlist. All services are subject to availability.

4. Payments & Refunds

Payment is required to secure your booking. We offer a full refund if you cancel before your first session. If you need to cancel within 48 hours of a session, a partial refund will be issued. No refunds will be provided after a session has taken place. Package refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis for unused sessions.

5. Results Disclaimer

Individual results may vary — every journey is unique and unfolds in its own time. Shining Clarity Coaching does not guarantee specific outcomes. You are the star of your own story, and we are here to help you find your orbit.

6. Confidentiality

What you share in sessions is held in confidence. We will not share your personal information with third parties except where required by law.

7. Our Right to End a Coaching Relationship

Shining Clarity Coaching reserves the right to discontinue services with any client at any time if we believe the coaching relationship is no longer appropriate. A prorated refund for unused sessions will be issued in such cases.
