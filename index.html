Our Story

Two coaches. One experience built around you.

Scott and Cait work with every client together — bringing different strengths to the same table so nothing gets missed.

Most people come to coaching after trying everything else. The books, the podcasts, the journaling, the therapy. And they're still stuck. Not because they haven't tried hard enough — but because they've been working on the symptoms instead of the source. Scott and Cait work differently. Scott identifies the behavior patterns and belief chains that are keeping you locked in place. Cait identifies the thinking patterns — the distortions, the loops, the "I can't" that sounds like fact but isn't — and interrupts them at the root. Together, they work with you simultaneously, from two angles, on the same session. You don't get one lens. You get two. And that's what actually moves the needle.

Scott

Scott has spent nearly two decades helping people see what they can't see on their own — the recurring patterns, the broken links in the belief chain, the behaviors that feel like personality but are actually just practice. He calls it finding the "wonky link." His work is precise, practical, and grounded. He doesn't just point to the problem. He helps you understand exactly where the chain broke and how to reset it.

Cait

Cait's coaching is built on lived experience — not the kind that comes from reading about something, but the kind that comes from doing the hardest version of it herself. She rebuilt her sense of worth and rewired her own core beliefs while still inside a relationship that actively worked against that. She knows what it looks like to be stuck inside the hard thing — not just after you've gotten out. That's what makes her different. And that's what makes her dangerous to your loops.

Together

Every client gets both of us — in the same session, working the same problem from different angles at the same time. Scott tracks what you do. Cait tracks how you think. Together we triangulate why — and that's where the real work happens. You don't have to choose between structure and empathy. That's kind of the whole point.

Our Mission

To find where you're stuck, disrupt the belief driving it, and build a clear path forward — with two coaches working alongside you at the same time.

Our Vision

A world where people stop managing their loops and actually break them. Where "this is just how I am" gets replaced with "this is what I believed — and I don't anymore."

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is coaching and how is it different from therapy?
Therapy looks at the past to heal what happened. Coaching looks at what's happening now and builds a path forward. We work on the beliefs and patterns keeping you stuck today — not diagnosing, just disrupting.
Why do both coaches work with me at the same time?
Because your patterns show up in more than one way. Scott tracks what you do — the behaviors and loops. Cait tracks how you think — the distortions, the stories, the "I can't" that sounds like truth. Working both angles in the same session is what makes real change stick.
How do I know which service is right for me?
Start with a free discovery call — that's exactly what it's for. We'll talk through what's going on and figure out the best fit together. No pressure, no commitment.
How do I get started?
Fill out the application on our Apply page and we'll be in touch to schedule your free discovery call.
What does a session look like?
You talk. We listen — both of us, for different things. Scott's tracking the patterns in what you're describing. Cait's tracking the thinking underneath it. Then we work together to find the root belief driving the loop and start disrupting it. It's real conversation, not a script.
I've tried coaching before and it didn't work. Why would this be different?
Most coaching works on behavior — what you do. We work on belief — why you do it. If the root belief doesn't change, the behavior comes back. That's why things haven't stuck before. That's what we fix.
Do you offer packages?
Yes — our Custom Coaching Package is designed around you. After your discovery call we do an initial audit, map your specific loop, and build a program around that. Pricing is based on your custom program.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a full refund if you cancel before your first session. If you need to cancel within 48 hours of a session, a partial refund will be issued. We want this to feel safe and fair for everyone.

Still have questions? Reach out — we're happy to help.
