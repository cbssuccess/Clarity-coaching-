Our Story

Two coaches. One experience built to help you see the pattern and shift it.

Most people don't need more advice. They need help seeing the loop they're in while also feeling supported enough to change it. That's why Scott and Cait work side by side — one tracking the pattern, one tracking the person, both helping you move forward.

Scott

Scott has spent 18 years helping people identify the TFBB link — the connection between thoughts, feelings, beliefs, and behaviors that keeps life repeating itself. He sees the pattern under the pattern.

Cait

Cait's coaching comes from living the hard thing. She knows what it is to build worthiness while you're still in the middle of the mess. She helps clients stay with themselves long enough to hear what's true.

Together

Every client gets both of us, at the same time. Scott tracks the pattern. Cait tracks the emotional truth. You don't have to choose between insight and support — you get both.

Our Mission

To create a space where people feel truly seen, heard, and understood — and walk away with the clarity, courage, and direction to take their next step forward.

Our Vision

A world where no one stays stuck at the crossroads alone. Where every person knows they have the power within them to align with who they are.
