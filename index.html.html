Shining Clarity Coaching is a coaching practice, not a therapy or mental health service. Cait & Scott are not licensed therapists, psychologists, or medical professionals. Coaching is not a substitute for professional mental health treatment.

We work with clients who are past the acute phase of crisis and ready to focus on clarity, direction, and rebuilding. If you are currently in active crisis or need mental health support, we will help connect you with appropriate resources.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please seek support from a qualified professional immediately.

Individual results vary — every journey unfolds in its own time.
