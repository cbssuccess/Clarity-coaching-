Our Story

Two coaches. One experience built to help you see the pattern and shift it.

Scott and Cait coach every client together — two lenses, one conversation, and a deeper level of support than either approach alone.

Most people don't need more advice. They need help seeing the loop they're in while also feeling supported enough to change it. That's why Scott and Cait work side by side — one questioning and uncovering the pattern, the other holding space and reflecting back what they're noticing. Together, they create a specific kind of clarity that one coach alone can't offer.

Scott

Scott has spent 18 years helping people identify the TFBB link — the wonky connection between thoughts, feelings, beliefs, and behaviors that keeps life repeating itself. He sees the pattern underneath every loop and asks the questions that create shifts. Scott brings structure, systems thinking, and the ability to see what others miss.

Cait

Cait's coaching comes from living the hard thing, not talking about it from a distance. She knows what it is to build worthiness while you're still in the middle of the mess — not after it ends, but now. Cait brings intuition, presence, and deep empathy. She meets clients in their humanity.

Together

You get a conversation with both of us. We design your coaching experience around you — your patterns, your timeline, your life. What we know is this: the people who change are the people who feel deeply seen and supported while they do it. That's what we offer.

Our Mission

To create a space where people feel truly seen, heard, and understood — and walk away with the clarity, courage, and direction to take their next step forward.

Our Vision

A world where no one stays stuck at the crossroads alone. Where every person knows they have the power within them to align with who they are, rise into who they're becoming, and move through life with integrity and ease.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is coaching and how is it different from therapy?
Coaching is forward focused. We don't dig into your past — we work with where you are now and where you want to go. We're not clinical, and we're not trying to heal trauma. We're focused on clarity, direction, and behavioral change.
How do I know which service is right for me?
Start with a free discovery call — that's exactly what it's for. We'll talk through what's going on, understand your goals, and recommend what makes sense for your situation.
How do I get started?
Book a discovery call through Setmore using the link on our Services page. After your call, we'll discuss next steps and pricing based on your needs.
Why are spots limited?
Because this work only works when there's enough space to do it well. We keep client spots limited so every person gets the full attention and care they deserve.
I've tried coaching before and it didn't work. Why would this be different?
Because we don't start with generic advice. You get both of us in every session. We're trained to see the invisible loop underneath your behavior, and we create real shifts by working at the belief level, not just the surface.
Do you offer packages?
Yes — our Loop Breaker Method (6–8 weeks) and Identity Rewire Experience (10–12 weeks) are our core packages. Both are customized to you based on your discovery call.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a full refund if you cancel before your first session. If you need to cancel within 48 hours of a scheduled session, please let us know as soon as possible.
What are your business hours?
We work with clients across time zones. Availability varies — we'll share current open times when you book your discovery call.

Still have questions? Reach out — we're happy to help.
